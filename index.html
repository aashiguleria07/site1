Welcome to Palampur

Palampur, known as the 'Tea Capital of North India', is situated amidst the majestic Dhauladhar mountain range. The town derives its name from the local word 'pulum', meaning 'lots of water'.

It is a popular tourist destination due to its cool, pleasant climate and the stunning backdrop of snow-capped mountains. The town offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and colonial architecture.

Travel Information

By Air

Gaggal Airport (Dharamshala) is the closest, approximately 40 km away.

By Rail

The narrow-gauge Kangra Valley Railway connects Palampur to Pathankot.

By Road

Well connected by National Highway 154 (NH 154) with regular HRTC bus services.
