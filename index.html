Discover Palampur, Himachal Pradesh

Immerse yourself in lush tea gardens, serene rivers, and rich culture. Your gateway to Himalayan bliss.

Explore Places

Top Tourist Places in Palampur

Tea Gardens

Palampur's iconic tea estates offer scenic walks, tea tasting, and panoramic views of the Kangra Valley. Best visited during harvest season.

  • Activities: Trekking, photography
  • Best Time: March to May

Neugal Khad

A pristine river with crystal-clear waters, ideal for picnics, rafting, and birdwatching. Surrounded by pine forests.

  • Activities: Rafting, fishing
  • Best Time: Year-round

Chamunda Devi Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Chamunda, perched on a hill with stunning views. A spiritual and historical site.

  • Activities: Worship, sightseeing
  • Best Time: October to March

Andretta (Art Village)

A quaint village known for pottery, paintings, and the Andretta Pottery & Craft Centre. Home to artist Norah Richards.

  • Activities: Workshops, art tours
  • Best Time: April to June

Saurabh Van Vihar

A lush botanical garden with Japanese-style landscaping, fountains, and picnic spots. Perfect for relaxation.

  • Activities: Walking, picnics
  • Best Time: March to June

Bundla Stream

A seasonal waterfall and stream with natural pools for swimming. Surrounded by deodar trees and meadows.

  • Activities: Swimming, trekking
  • Best Time: July to September

Birni Temple

A 13th-century temple with intricate carvings, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Offers historical insights and serene vibes.

  • Activities: Exploration, photography
  • Best Time: October to May

Tatwani Hot Springs

Natural sulfur springs believed to have healing properties. A unique spot for relaxation and wellness.

  • Activities: Bathing, sightseeing
  • Best Time: Year-round

About Palampur

Palampur, nestled in the Kangra Valley, is renowned for its tea plantations, colonial architecture, and proximity to Dharamshala. It's a haven for nature lovers, offering a blend of adventure, culture, and tranquility.
