Palampur is a beautiful hill station in Kangra Valley, famous for tea gardens, pine forests, colonial architecture, and scenic trekking routes. It offers a peaceful environment with stunning mountain views.

How to Reach

  • By Road: Well connected with Dharamshala, Kangra & Pathankot
  • By Rail: Nearest railhead is Pathankot (narrow gauge)
  • By Air: Gaggal Airport (40 km)
