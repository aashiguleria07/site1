Palampur

The Tea Capital of Himachal Pradesh

An Overview

Palampur, known as the "Tea Capital of North India," is nestled in the foothills of the Dhauladhar mountain range. It is famous for its lush green tea gardens, peaceful environment, and beautiful panoramic views. This place offers a unique blend of art, culture, and nature.

The name Palampur is derived from the word 'Pulam', meaning 'lots of water'. The climate here is pleasant throughout the year, making it an ideal holiday destination.

How to Reach

By Air

The nearest airport is Gaggal Airport (Dharamshala) in Kangra, which is about 40 km away.

By Rail

Palampur has a narrow-gauge railway station connected to Pathankot.

By Road

Palampur is connected via National Highway 154 (NH 154) with regular bus services available from major cities in the state.
